Project Introduction: CuraBook
CuraBook is a web application designed to streamline the process of scheduling and managing medical appointments. The project aims to provide users with an intuitive and efficient platform for booking healthcare appointments at various medical facilities.

The target audience for the application includes patients seeking a quick and easy way to schedule appointments with their healthcare providers and medical facilities that want to optimize the scheduling process and enhance the overall patient experience.

Key features of the application include:

Selecting a medical facility and type of healthcare service.
Booking an appointment based on the patientâ€™s preferences.
Providing specific requests or comments related to the visit.
Managing existing appointments, including options to modify or cancel them.
Sending reminders and confirmations for appointments.
CuraBook is designed to be user-friendly for both tech-savvy users and those who prefer simplicity and clarity. The project's goal is to improve access to healthcare and enhance patient satisfaction through a modern and intuitive booking system.
